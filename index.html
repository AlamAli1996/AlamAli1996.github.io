About Me

Alam Ali is currently working as a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at IIT Kanpur, Kanpur, India. He completed his Ph.D. in 2025 from the Central University of Punjab, Bathinda, India. He earned his Master’s degree in 2018 and Bachelor’s degree in 2016, both from the University of Lucknow, Lucknow, India. His research interests include copulas and regression modeling. Recently, he has developed an interest in regression modeling using copulas. This position allows him to learn the expertise in his field and make meaningful contributions to academia.
